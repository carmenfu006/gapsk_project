收集個人資料聲明

GAPSK非常重視個人私隱，並確保轄下之服務於任何情況下收集、使用、儲存、轉移及查閱個人資料之程序均符合香港的《個人資料(私隱)條例》的要求。我們將確保我們的職員嚴格遵守本收集個人資料聲明及我們的私隱聲明（私隱政策）。請細閱下文以了解我們的收集個人資料政策。

收集資料的目的

GAPSK會使用你提供的個人資料，向申請人提供申請人所需要的考試、比賽、活動、課程或服務，包括參與考試、比賽、活動、課程或服務的相關用途、簽發收據、收集意見及資料分析。同時亦會使用申請人提供的個人資料向申請人提供有關香港青年協會及有關單位的相關考試、比賽、活動、課程、服務及捐獻要求等推廣資訊，申請人可隨時向GAPSK表明或更改接收推廣及宣傳意願。閣下有責任提供申請表格上列為「必填」的資料，或啟動相關流程必須提供的資料，否則GAPSK有可能無法提供閣下要求之服務。

資料的轉移

申請人所提供的個人資料，會供GAPSK在工作上有需要知道該等資料的職員或指定人士使用。GAPSK不會租用、出售、轉移或披露所持有之個人資料予他人或非GAPSK有關單位，除非：

  1. 對方為於業務上向GAPSK提供服務的代理機構、承辦商或服務提供者；
  2. 閣下要求之服務由有關人士／公司負責提供；
  3. 已預先得到資料當事人的同意；
  4. 對非法活動、懷疑詐騙、涉及或威脅到任何人的人身安全的事件作出調查、預防及採取行動；
  5. 遵循所有適用法津、規定、法律程序、具法律效力的政府要求、行政制度或規例要求。

查閱及更改個人資料

申請人請確保向GAPSK提供的資料正確無誤，並有責任向GAPSK更新資料，否則GAPSK有可能無法提供閣下要求之服務。申請人有權要求查閱和改正所提供的個人資料及索取有關資料的複本。如需查詢或改正其個人資料，可以書面形式申請，並電郵至info@gapsk.org。GAPSK在處理查閱或改正資料要求時，會查核提出要求者的身份，以確保他／她在法律上有權作出這項要求。在收到閣下提出的要求後，GAPSK將在 40 天內給予回覆，GAPSK將可能就此收取合理的費用；GAPSK須在或可在《個人資料(私隱)修例》第 20 條指明的情況下拒絕依從查閱資料要求，例如申請人未能提供GAPSK合理要求的資訊。

Personal Data Collection Statement

GAPSK attaches great importance to personal privacy and ensures that the procedures for collecting, using, storing, transferring and accessing personal data of its services comply with the requirements of Hong Kong's Personal Data (Privacy) Ordinance under any circumstances. We will ensure that our staff strictly comply with this Personal Information Collection Statement and our privacy statement (Privacy Policy). Please read below to understand our policy on collecting personal data.

Purpose of collecting information

GAPSK will use the personal information you provide to provide applicants with the examinations, competitions, activities, courses or services required by the applicant, including related purposes of participating in examinations, competitions, activities, courses or services, issuing receipts, collecting opinions and information analyze. At the same time, the personal information provided by the applicant will also be used to provide the applicant with promotional information about relevant examinations, competitions, activities, courses, services and donation requirements of the Hong Kong Federation of Youth Groups and relevant units. Applicants can indicate or change the receipt of promotions to GAPSK at any time and willingness to publicize. You are responsible for providing the information listed as "required" on the application form, or the information required to start the relevant process, otherwise GAPSK may not be able to provide the services you request.

Transfer of data

The personal information provided by the applicant will be used by GAPSK staff or designated persons who need to know the information for their work. GAPSK will not rent, sell, transfer or disclose the personal information it holds to others or non-GAPSK related entities unless:

  1. The other party is an agency, contractor or service provider that provides business services to GAPSK;
  2. The services you request are provided by the relevant person/company;
  3. The consent of the data subject has been obtained in advance;
  4. Investigate, prevent and take action regarding illegal activities, suspected fraud, or incidents involving or threatening the physical safety of any person;
  5. Comply with all applicable laws, regulations, legal procedures, legally binding governmental requests, administrative systems or regulatory requirements.

Access and change personal information

Applicants please ensure that the information provided to GAPSK is correct and are responsible for updating the information to GAPSK, otherwise GAPSK may not be able to provide the services you request. Applicants have the right to request access to and correction of the personal data provided and to obtain a copy of the relevant data. If you need to inquire or correct your personal data, you can apply in writing and email to info@gapsk.org. When GAPSK processes a request for data access or correction, it will check the identity of the person making the request to ensure that he/she is legally entitled to make the request. After receiving your request, GAPSK will respond within 40 days, and GAPSK may charge a reasonable fee; GAPSK shall or may be required to do so under the circumstances specified in Article 20 of the Personal Data (Privacy) Amendment Act Refusal to comply with a data access request, for example if the applicant fails to provide information reasonably requested by GAPSK.
