私隱政策聲明

GAPSK非常重視個人私隱，並確保轄下之服務於任何情況下收集、使用、儲存、轉移及查閱個人資料之程序均符合香港的《個人資料(私隱)條例》的要求。本聲明旨在說明GAPSK收集個人資料的政策及原則。

收集資料

閣下在使用GAPSK及有關單位之服務、為GAPSK提供服務、受僱於GAPSK、作出投訴及／或瀏覽GAPSK各個網頁時，GAPSK將可能向閣下收集個人資料，包括但不限於以下資料：

活動參加者／服務使用者：姓名、電話號碼、手提電話號碼、地址、電郵地址、出生日期、身份證號碼、監護人資料、就讀學校等。

服務提供者：姓名、身份證號碼、電話號碼、地址、銀行戶口號碼等。

投訴人：投訴人姓名、電話、電郵、投訴內容、調查過程及結果等。

閣下有責任提供申請表格上列為「必填」的資料，或啟動相關流程必須提供的資料，否則GAPSK有可能無法提供閣下要求之服務。

使用資料

活動參加者／服務使用者：有關資料用作參與活動的相關用途、簽發收據、收集意見、資料分析，及其他配合本會宗旨及使命的事項。

服務提供者：有關資料將用作發放合約酬金等事宜。

投訴人：有關資料將用作調查、回應及跟進相關投訴工作。

所有GAPSK及有關單位的服務資訊的傳遞將按《個人資料(私隱)條例》之要求，於取得資料當事人的同意才進行。資料當事人如需取消接收GAPSK及有關單位的資訊，請電郵至 info@gapsk.org GAPSK職員聯絡。

外判安排

GAPSK有部分服務由第三方服務供應商開發及／或提供，這些服務包括但不限於：開發及維修系統、提供班組活動等。

GAPSK所有服務供應商均受合約約束，必須將所有個人資料保密，GAPSK亦承諾採取一切可行的措施保障閣下的個人資料。

將個人資料轉移至香港以外地方

GAPSK會在必要時，將個人資料轉移至香港以外的地方，以達到收集個人資料之目的或與其有關之目的。

保留資料

GAPSK只在有必要達到收集閣下個人資料所訂明的用途期間內，保留閣下的個人資料。如GAPSK持有的個人資料無需再用於收集資料時所列出的目的，將在合理時間內銷毀有關資料。

披露個人資料

GAPSK不會租用、出售、轉移或披露GAPSK所持有之個人資料予他人或非GAPSK有關單位，除非：

  1. 對方為於業務上向GAPSK提供行政、電訊、電腦或其他任何服務的人士、代理機構、承辦商或服務提供者；
  2. 閣下要求之服務由有關人士／公司負責提供；
  3. 已預先得到資料當事人的同意；
  4. 對非法活動、懷疑詐騙、涉及或威脅到任何人的人身安全的事件作出調查、預防及採取行動；
  5. 遵循所有適用法津、規定、法律程序、具法律效力的政府要求、行政制度或規例要求。

瀏覽GAPSK網頁及使用GAPSK手機程式時被收集的資料

一般使用者到訪GAPSK網頁或使用GAPSK手機程式時，為令使用更暢順及更方便，有一些基本資料會被自動存取，例如IP地址及／或域名、瀏覽器類別及設定、語言設定、地區、操作系統、及瀏覽時間或其他有關資料，以上資料均為不記名，在一般情況下並不能辨識瀏覽者的身份。當需要可辨識個人身份的資料時，網站及手機應用程式將會有明確的提示及讓使用者同意方會進行。

保障措施

GAPSK採取適當措施以保障GAPSK持有的個人資料，免受遺失、未經許可的收集、使用、修改、轉移、查閱及披露。

GAPSK各網頁的登記用戶有責任確保帳號及密碼的機密及安全。用戶需對利用有關帳號及密碼所進行的一切行動，負完全的責任。

查閱及改正資料

閣下有責任向GAPSK更新資料，否則GAPSK有可能無法提供閣下要求之服務。

根據《個人資料(私隱)修例》，資料當事人有權要求查閱和改正所提供的個人資料及索取有關資料的複本。GAPSK在處理查閱或改正資料要求時，會查核提出要求者的身份，以確保他／她在法律上有權作出這項要求。GAPSK須在或可在條例第20條指明的情況下拒絕依從查閱資料要求，例如申請人未能提供GAPSK合理要求的資訊。

閣下如需查詢或改正其個人資料，可以書面形式申請並電郵至 info@gapsk.org。在收到閣下提出的要求後，GAPSK將在40天內給予回覆，GAPSK將可能就此收取合理的費用。

Privacy Policy Statement

GAPSK attaches great importance to personal privacy and ensures that the procedures for collecting, using, storing, transferring and accessing personal data of its services comply with the requirements of Hong Kong's Personal Data (Privacy) Ordinance under any circumstances. This statement is intended to explain GAPSK's policies and principles for collecting personal information.

Collect information

When you use the services of GAPSK and related entities, provide services to GAPSK, are employed by GAPSK, make complaints and/or browse various GAPSK web pages, GAPSK may collect personal information from you, including but not limited to the following information:

Activity participants/service users: name, phone number, mobile phone number, address, email address, date of birth, ID number, guardian information, school attended, etc.

Service provider: name, ID number, phone number, address, bank account number, etc.

Complainant: Complainant’s name, phone number, email, complaint content, investigation process and results, etc.

You are responsible for providing the information listed as "required" on the application form, or the information required to start the relevant process, otherwise GAPSK may not be able to provide the services you request.

Usage information

Event participants/service users: Relevant data will be used for activities related purposes, issuance of receipts, collection of opinions, data analysis, and other matters in line with the association's aims and missions.

Service Provider: Relevant information will be used for payment of contract remuneration and other matters.

Complainant: The relevant information will be used for investigation, response and follow-up of related complaints.

All transmission of service information of GAPSK and related units will be carried out with the consent of the data subject in accordance with the requirements of the Personal Data (Privacy) Ordinance. If the data subject needs to cancel receiving information from GAPSK and related organizations, please email GAPSK staff at info@gapsk.org.

Outsourcing arrangement

Some services of GAPSK are developed and/or provided by third-party service providers. These services include but are not limited to: developing and maintaining systems, providing team activities, etc.

All service providers of GAPSK are bound by contracts and must keep all personal information confidential. GAPSK also promises to take all feasible measures to protect your personal information.

Transfer of personal data outside Hong Kong

GAPSK will transfer personal data to places outside Hong Kong when necessary to achieve the purpose of collecting personal data or for purposes related to it.

Retain information

GAPSK will retain your personal information only as long as it is necessary to fulfill the purpose for which it was collected. If the personal data held by GAPSK is no longer needed for the purposes listed at the time of collection, the relevant data will be destroyed within a reasonable time.

Disclosure of personal information

GAPSK will not rent, sell, transfer or disclose personal information held by GAPSK to others or non-GAPSK related entities unless:

  1. The other party is a person, agency, contractor or service provider that provides administrative, telecommunications, computer or any other services to GAPSK in business;
  2. The services you request are provided by the relevant person/company;
  3. The consent of the data subject has been obtained in advance;
  4. Investigate, prevent and take action regarding illegal activities, suspected fraud, or incidents involving or threatening the physical safety of any person;
  5. Comply with all applicable laws, regulations, legal procedures, legally binding governmental requests, administrative systems or regulatory requirements.

Information collected when browsing the GAPSK website and using the GAPSK mobile app

When general users visit the GAPSK website or use the GAPSK mobile app, some basic information will be automatically accessed to make the use smoother and more convenient, such as IP address and/or domain name, browser type and settings, and language settings. , region, operating system, and browsing time or other relevant information. The above information is anonymous and cannot identify the visitor under normal circumstances. When personally identifiable information is required, the website and mobile application will provide clear prompts and allow users to consent before proceeding.

Safety precautions

GAPSK takes appropriate measures to protect the personal information held by GAPSK from loss, unauthorized collection, use, modification, transfer, access and disclosure.

Registered users of each GAPSK website are responsible for ensuring the confidentiality and security of their account numbers and passwords. Users are fully responsible for all actions taken using the relevant account and password.

Access and correct information

You are responsible for updating information to GAPSK, otherwise GAPSK may not be able to provide the services you request.

Under the Personal Data (Privacy) Amendment Bill, data subjects have the right to request access to and correction of the personal data provided and to obtain a copy of the relevant data. When GAPSK processes a request for data access or correction, it will check the identity of the person making the request to ensure that he/she is legally entitled to make the request. GAPSK must or may refuse to comply with a data access request in the circumstances specified in section 20 of the Ordinance, such as where the applicant fails to provide information reasonably requested by GAPSK.

If you need to inquire or correct your personal data, you may apply in writing and email to info@gapsk.org. After receiving your request, GAPSK will respond within 40 days, and GAPSK may charge a reasonable fee for this.
